Our Features

At Shipdartexpress, we understand the importance of flexibility and convenience when it comes to making payments for your shipments. That's why we offer Cash On Delivery (COD) as a hassle-free payment option for both senders and recipients. With COD, you can enjoy the convenience of paying for your shipments upon delivery, ensuring peace of mind and satisfaction with our services.

How Does Cash On Delivery Work?

Our Cash On Delivery service is designed to simplify the payment process for your shipments. Here's how it works:

  • Sender's Choice: As the sender, you have the option to select Cash On Delivery as the payment method when booking your shipment.
  • Shipment Delivery: We will pick up your shipment and deliver it to the recipient's address.
  • Payment Upon Delivery: When the shipment reaches its destination, our courier will collect the payment amount specified by you in cash or through another agreed-upon payment method.
  • Secure Transaction: Our couriers are trained to handle COD transactions securely and professionally, ensuring that your payment is safely collected and recorded.

Our COD service is designed to offer convenience and peace of mind to both senders and recipients. It allows for easy verification of the shipment's contents before making the payment.

Benefits of Cash On Delivery

Choosing Cash On Delivery as your payment method with Shipdartexpress comes with several benefits:

  • Payment Convenience: Recipients can pay for their shipments at the time of delivery, eliminating the need for advance payments.
  • Security and Trust: COD provides an extra layer of security, as payments are made only upon verifying the contents of the shipment.
  • Flexibility: Senders have the flexibility to offer COD as a payment option to cater to recipient preferences.
  • Reduced Risk: With COD, senders can reduce the risk of non-payment and ensure that the recipient is satisfied with the shipment.

Using Cash On Delivery with Shipdartexpress

To use our COD service, simply select it as your preferred payment method when booking your shipment. Specify the payment amount to be collected upon delivery, and our courier will handle the rest. You can trust Shipdartexpress to facilitate a smooth and secure COD transaction for your shipment.

Additional Information

Our Cash On Delivery service is subject to specific terms and conditions, which may vary based on the nature of the shipment and the destination. It is essential to review and agree to these terms when selecting COD as your payment method. Our COD remitance delivery will be done within 2 to 8 days.

Shipdartexpress makes the shipping process easy and efficient, and that includes the ability to print shipping labels conveniently from the comfort of your home or office. With our user-friendly online platform, you can generate and print shipping labels in just a few simple steps.

How to Print Shipping Labels with Shipdartexpress

Printing shipping labels with Shipdartexpress is a straightforward process that ensures your shipments are properly labeled and ready for delivery. Here's how you can do it:

  • Log into Your Shipdartexpress Account: If you don't have an account, you can create one quickly and easily.
  • Create a Shipment: Enter the shipment details, including the sender's and recipient's addresses, package dimensions, and weight.
  • Select Shipping Service: Choose the shipping service that suits your needs.
  • Generate Shipping Label: Once you've completed the shipment details, click the "Generate Label" or "Print Label" button.
  • Print the Label: Your shipping label will be generated in a printable format. Ensure you have a printer ready and click "Print."
  • Attach the Label: Affix the printed shipping label securely to your package, ensuring it's visible and legible.

That's it! Your package is now properly labeled and ready for shipping with Shipdartexpress. Our system generates labels with all the necessary information, including tracking numbers, sender and recipient details, and barcodes for efficient tracking and delivery.

Benefits of Printing Shipping Labels with Shipdartexpress

Using Shipdartexpress to print your shipping labels offers several advantages:

  • Convenience: No need to visit a shipping center; you can print labels from your home or office.
  • Accuracy: Our platform ensures that all essential shipping information is included on the label, reducing the risk of errors.
  • Efficiency: Save time by generating and printing labels in a matter of minutes.
  • Tracking: Each label includes a unique tracking number, allowing you to monitor your shipment's progress.

Label Printing Tips

To ensure the best results when printing your shipping labels, consider the following tips:

  • Use a High-Quality Printer: A laser or thermal printer is ideal for printing shipping labels for clarity and durability.
  • Choose Label Paper: Use adhesive label paper designed for shipping labels to ensure they adhere securely to your packages.
  • Check Print Settings: Ensure your printer's settings are optimized for label printing, such as label size and orientation.
